Our company provides 20 paid annual leave days per calendar year to all full-time employees. Employees must submit leave requests at least three working days in advance through the internal HR portal. Emergency leave may be requested in urgent situations and requires manager approval. Sick leave is limited to 10 days annually and may require medical documentation for absences longer than two consecutive days. Employees are expected to maintain professional conduct, adhere to working hours from 9 AM to 6 PM, and comply with company data security guidelines.
